Featured Product

ParentShield

ParentShield is a privacy-first evidence logging app designed to help parents document incidents, keep records organized, and create more credible exports in high-conflict parenting situations.

Built by Ninja Riot Studio with a local-first mindset, ParentShield is designed to keep records under the user's control with no cloud storage and no forced account system.
