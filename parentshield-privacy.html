ParentShield Privacy Policy

ParentShield is an independent tool developed by Ninja Riot Studio.

ParentShield is built around privacy, local control, and secure documentation. This app is designed to help users create and manage personal records on their own device.

1. Data Collection

ParentShield does not collect, store, or transmit your personal records to Ninja Riot Studio servers. Records, notes, and related data are stored locally on your device.

2. Local Storage and Encryption

Sensitive data inside ParentShield is protected using a secure vault system. Access to the vault is controlled by the password created by the user.

You are responsible for maintaining access to your vault credentials, including your password and any recovery materials provided by the app. If those credentials are lost, your data may not be recoverable.

3. Attachments

Photos and videos attached to records may be stored locally on your device and linked within the app. Some attachment files may remain unencrypted on disk, but integrity protections are used to help detect tampering.

4. Data Sharing

ParentShield does not sell, rent, or share your personal records with third parties. Your data only leaves your device when you choose to export or share it.

5. Exported Files

ParentShield may generate exported files such as PDF reports and ZIP bundles on your device. You are responsible for how those exported files are stored, transmitted, or shared after export.

6. Internet Use

ParentShield does not require an internet connection for its core recordkeeping functionality. The app is designed to function primarily as a local-first tool.

7. Children's Information

ParentShield does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Any information entered into the app is entered by the user.

8. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated over time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or clarification of practices. Updates will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For questions about ParentShield or this privacy policy, please use the Contact page on the Ninja Riot Studio website.
