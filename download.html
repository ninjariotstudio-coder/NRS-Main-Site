Your support helps fund ParentShield development and gives you access to the current early access version.

Your Download Will Be Delivered

ParentShield is currently delivered through private or manual access after purchase rather than through a public website download.

This helps protect the build while development is still active and keeps distribution more controlled during early access.

After purchase, your download link or APK file will be provided directly by Ninja Riot Studio.

Contact for Delivery Support

If you do not receive your delivery details, use the contact page and include the email used for purchase.

What Happens Next

  1. Complete your ParentShield early access purchase through Stripe.
  2. Your APK file or private download link will be sent manually or provided through private delivery.
  3. Download the APK to your Android device.
  4. Open the APK from your device and follow Android's install prompts.
  5. If Android blocks the install, allow the install from that source when prompted, then continue setup.

Early Access Notes

ParentShield is an early access build under active development. Features, design, and functionality may change over time.

This version is distributed directly by Ninja Riot Studio before public Google Play release.

Purchase provides access to the current early access version and does not grant ownership, partnership, or decision-making authority in Ninja Riot Studio or ParentShield.

Need Help?

If you have trouble receiving your build, installing the APK, or opening ParentShield, contact Ninja Riot Studio and we will help you get set up.
