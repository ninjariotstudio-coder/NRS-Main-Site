Website Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Ninja Riot Studio respects your privacy. This Website Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected through this website, how it may be used, and how we work to protect it.

This policy applies to the Ninja Riot Studio website only. It does not replace the privacy policy for any individual app or product, including ParentShield.

1. Information You May Provide

If you use forms on this website, such as contact forms, tester sign-up forms, or review submission forms, you may provide information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Feedback or message content
  • Testing interest or device/platform information

2. How That Information Is Used

Information submitted through this website may be used to:

  • Respond to messages or support requests
  • Contact potential testers
  • Review feedback or submitted reviews
  • Improve Ninja Riot Studio products, pages, and services

Submitted information is used only for legitimate communication, testing, support, and product improvement purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This website may use trusted third-party services to operate certain features. For example, form submissions may be handled through a service such as Formspree, and donation links may direct to third-party platforms such as GoFundMe or PayPal.

When you interact with those services, their own privacy policies and terms may apply.

4. Cookies and Basic Analytics

This website may use basic hosting, performance, or security technologies that collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, or pages visited. This information is used to keep the site running and improve the experience.

Ninja Riot Studio does not sell personal information.

5. Data Sharing

Personal information submitted through this website is not sold or rented. Information may only be shared when necessary to operate the website, process form submissions, comply with law, or protect the rights and safety of Ninja Riot Studio and its users.

6. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect submitted information, but no website or online service can guarantee absolute security. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive information through website forms unless specifically requested and appropriate to do so.

7. Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and Ninja Riot Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

8. Product-Specific Policies

Individual apps or services may have their own privacy policies. ParentShield, for example, has a separate privacy policy that applies to the app itself.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Website Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the website evolves. Updated versions will be posted on this page with the latest effective date.
