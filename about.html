Built with purpose. Designed for protection.

Ninja Riot Studio is an independent software company focused on building privacy-first, security-first tools for real-world situations.

Our Story

Ninja Riot Studio was created to build software that actually helps people — not software that tracks them, sells their data, or overwhelms them with noise.

This studio was built on the belief that people deserve tools that protect them, respect their privacy, and serve a real purpose.

Why We Build

Too many tools today are designed around engagement, data collection, and keeping users dependent on platforms they do not control.

Ninja Riot Studio takes a different approach: build tools that respect the user, protect their information, and help them stay organized, prepared, and in control.

Why Ninja Riot Studio Exists

Ninja Riot Studio was built on one simple belief: people deserve tools that protect them — not exploit them.

Our products are created from real-world problems, not boardroom trends. That means focusing on privacy, trust, security, and practical value first.

No data harvesting. No manipulation. No hidden agendas. Just software built to help when it matters most.

What “Security-First” Means

Data Protection

We design systems to minimize and protect sensitive information.

Intentional Design

Security is part of the architecture, not an afterthought.

User Control

Users should control their data, not the platform.

Transparency

We are clear about how our tools work and what they do.
